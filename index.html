Thank you for visiting my page! Hope you liked my game :)

Rules

  1. Black starts the game.
  2. Pieces only move forwards by one.
  3. To capture a piece, you move diagonally by one. You can jump multiple times.
  4. You win when your opponenent cannot make a move.
